❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Style Of Life
🛍️ How do I place an order?

Browse our shop, add items to cart, proceed to checkout, enter shipping details, and confirm order. You'll receive confirmation via email.

💳 What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments via Razorpay (Credit/Debit Cards, NetBanking, UPI) and Cash on Delivery (COD).

🚚 How long does shipping take?

Chennai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Patna: 5-7 business days. Other cities: 7-10 business days.

💰 Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! Free shipping on all orders above ₹3,000. For orders below ₹3,000, flat ₹100 shipping fee applies.

🔄 Can I return or exchange my jewelry?

Style Of Life does not accept returns or exchanges. All sales are final. For damaged products, contact us within 24 hours.

🔍 How can I track my order?

Use the Track Order page with your Order ID to see real-time status.
