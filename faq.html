❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Style Of Life
🛍️ How do I place an order?

Placing an order is easy:

  1. Browse our shop and select your favorite jewelry
  2. Click "Add to Cart" or "Buy Now"
  3. Review your cart and proceed to checkout
  4. Enter shipping details and choose payment method
  5. Confirm and place your order

You'll receive an order confirmation via email.

💳 What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments through Razorpay:

  • Razorpay: Credit/Debit Cards, NetBanking, UPI, Wallets

All payments are 100% secure and encrypted.

🚚 How long does shipping take?

Delivery timelines based on your location:

  • Metro Cities: 5-7 business days
  • Tier 2 & 3 Cities: 7-10 business days
  • Remote Locations: 10-12 business days

Orders are processed within 24-48 hours after payment confirmation.

💰 Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free shipping on all orders above ₹3,000.

For orders below ₹3,000, a flat shipping fee of ₹100 applies.

🔄 Can I return or exchange my jewelry?

Style Of Life does not accept returns or exchanges. All sales are final.

However, if you receive a damaged or defective product:

  • Contact us within 24 hours of delivery
  • Provide clear photos of the damage
  • We will assess and provide resolution (repair/replacement at our discretion)
🔍 How can I track my order?

You can track your order by:

  • Clicking the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
  • Visiting our Track Order page
  • Entering your Order ID to see real-time status
🛡️ Is my payment information secure?

Yes! We use Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway.

  • All transactions are encrypted with SSL/TLS
  • We never store your card details
  • Two-factor authentication for added security

Yes! All orders come in secure, elegant packaging perfect for gifting.

You can also add a personalized gift message at checkout (free of cost).

🧼 How do I care for my jewelry?

To keep your jewelry shining:

  • Avoid contact with perfumes, lotions, and chemicals
  • Remove before swimming or bathing
  • Clean with a soft cloth after each wear
  • Store in a safe, dry place
  • Get professional cleaning annually
